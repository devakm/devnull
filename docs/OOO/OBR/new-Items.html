New Items — cloned and retextured armor / weapon sets

OOORF ships a family of recolored Glass / Mithril / Elven / Leather / Fur armor sets — and an Obsidian weapon line — produced by the OblivionRemastered_ItemClone pipeline. Each set lives on its own UE5 item assets (cloned from a vanilla donor and retextured), bound to existing OOO ESP records via the SyncMap. This page lists every shipped set with its inventory icons, EDIDs, FormIDs, and UE5 asset paths. Source data: Oscuro's_Oblivion_Overhaul_Overrides.ini for paths; OOO ARMO/WEAP.json for in-game names.
